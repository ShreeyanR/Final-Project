General knowledge quizzes often cover topics like history, science, geography, and current events. The difficulty of the questions depends on the intended audience. Math quizzes can help students learn and practice math skills. They can be used to assess math proficiency, prepare for exams, and compete in math competitions. A "science quiz" is a set of questions designed to test a person's knowledge about various scientific concepts and facts.
